Reed has experience with both commercial off-the-shelf hardware and software, as well as specialized applications in an enterprise environment. He has over 3 years of experience in network-cloud operations, including deployment and design. He has worked with the most notable companies in the industry including IBM and Microsoft. He has 10 industry certifications, and a bachelor's degree in information systems. This site is a presentation of some of his work in the educational technology space.
